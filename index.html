FAQs

How do I get the ROXi app on my TV? +

If your TV or device is listed here, then you’ll find the ROXi app by searching the app store on your device.
Please note that Samsung TV models must be from 2018 onwards. Any smart TV or device that runs on the Google TV or Android TV operating
system will support the ROXi app

If you don’t currently have a compatible smart TV or device, please register your interest, as we are releasing the ROXi app
on more platforms all the time

I’m not sure if my TV runs on Google TV or Android TV, how do I check? +

The ROXi app is available on any smart TV or device that runs on the Google TV or Android TV operating system, which lots
of brands do (including those listed below), but it depends on the model. If in doubt, please check with your TV or device
manufacturer. Brands that run on Google TV or Android TV: Sony Bravia, Panasonic, Toshiba, Hisense, JVC, Philips, Bush, Hitachi, Sharp, TCL, Nvidia Shield and others.

Brands that run on Google TV or Android TV: Sony Bravia, Panasonic, Toshiba, Hisense, JVC, Philips, Bush, Hitachi, Sharp, TCL,
Nvidia Shield and others.

Is the ROXi app free? +

Yes, the ROXi app is free to download and free to enjoy! You can choose between two plans: ROXi Standard, which is free
forever with adverts. Or ROXi Premium, which gives you all the music and features, with none of the adverts and is free for 30 days
(it’s just £6.99 a month after that and you can cancel anytime).

Can you use ROXi without a subscription? +

Yes, ROXi is free! As soon as you create your free account, you have access to 90 million music videos, karaoke and games on
your TV. You can expect to see some adverts and access to some of the content and features will be restricted, but there’s
no subscription required to enjoy ROXi. If you want access to all of the music and features with none of the adverts, you can
try ROXi Premium free for 30 days.

How do I get the ROXi Premium plan? +

First, create your free account, then follow the easy steps to claim your free 30-day trial of ROXi Premium with a free karaoke
mic (postage is just £1 and T&Cs apply). Or if you already have a ROXi account, you can easily upgrade online.

Is the ROXi Karaoke Mic really free and how do I get it? +

Yes! First, create your free account, then follow the easy steps to claim your free karaoke mic offer with a free 30-day trial of
ROXi Premium. The mic is free, it’s just £1 for postage and packaging

Is ROXi free on Sky Q and how do I get it? +

Yes, ROXi is free on Sky Q. Just head to ‘Apps’ and then scroll to the ‘Music’ rail, where you’ll find the ROXi app pre-installed.
There’s no need to download the app, so just open it, enter your email address and enjoy ROXi for free!

Is the ROXi app available on Sky Stream and Sky Glass, or just Sky Q? +

The ROXi app is currently only available to Sky customers with Sky Q but will be launching on Sky Stream and Sky Glass soon.
Register your interest if you’d like to be kept updated.

Can I use ROXi on my phone or tablet? +

Not yet! ROXi is currently only available on these smart TVs and streaming devices. However, you can use the ROXi
Companion App for iOS and Android to control the TV with your phone (which is particularly handy for voice search and
creating playlists!).
